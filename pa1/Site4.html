There are many aspects to web development. Some developers will only have knowledge in the front end aspects such as HTML, CSS and javascript. A full stack developer is like a jack of all trades having experience in in the front end, back end, databases and mobile app development.The full stack is essentially everything required to complete a project. This level of knowledge lends itself to full stack developers being payed well and in high demand in the web devlopment industries.

Photo taken from https://hackernoon.com/

Disadvantages

It's a very useful asset for a company to have a full stack developer. They will have a good idea of how to create a project due to the horizontal knowledge across technologies. They can also communicate with the entire team due to familiarity with a vast realm of technologies. The only issue is a full stack developer will not be an expert in any one field. So it might be misleading that since they are comfortable with many technologies that they will have the best solutions for a specific technology at a given time.
